Android App

Safe Droning

This project solves the aeronautics challenge: DON’T CRASH MY DRONE at the NASA Space Apps 2016. The aim was to create an app that will enable small drone operators to know more about specific weather parameters, local terrain and no fly zones within a five-mile radius of their GPS location. It uses a DIY drone built from scratch, Multiwii FC. 2.5, Crius NEO-6 GPS, I2C Board and RESTful APIs. This project taught me how to develop under pressure (the challenge duration was 2 days) and how to work with unknown people.
